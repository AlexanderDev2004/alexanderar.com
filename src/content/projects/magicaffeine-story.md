## What is this project?

In your past life, you were a coffee-loving barista, but now you've been thrust into a fantasy world. One day, longing for the familiar comfort of coffee, you try a local brew—only to find that something is terribly wrong with the taste. It's bland, bitter, and completely lacks the soul you once knew. Determined to bring real coffee to this foreign land, you decide to open your own coffee shop. Your mission: to share the rich, delicious flavors of coffee with the inhabitants of this new realm, and maybe, just maybe, spark a cultural revolution—one cup at a time.

## The Solution

This project demonstrates my ability to build games with a narrative feel and focused mechanics. The game was developed using Unity 2D and C#. The focus is on character-driven exploration as well as gameplay aspects that are relaxing yet still engaging for players.
