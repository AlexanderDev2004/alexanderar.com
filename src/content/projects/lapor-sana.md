## The Problem
Facility problems on campus are a common issue that students, faculty, and staff face. These problems can range from maintenance issues to safety concerns, and they can be challenging to address. Facility improvements are another area where the campus needs improvement. However, there is a lack of resources and expertise to make these improvements happen.

## The Solution
Laporsana is a website to create and manage facility problems on campus, facility improvements with the integration of a decision support system, which provides recommendations for solving existing problems on campus.
