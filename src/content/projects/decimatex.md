## The Problem
So, since in the fourth semester of my study, I encountered a Decision Support System course, I needed a fast and reliable way to calculate and make decisions. However, there was no available platform to perform these calculations. While AI tools sometimes failed to provide accurate answers, I decided to develop my own DSS platform to assist students, lecturers, and others in making effective decisions.

## The Solution
I decided to develop Decimatex, a Decision Support System (DSS) platform that automates decision analysis using MCDM (Multi-Criteria Decision Making) methods. The platform is designed to be user-friendly, efficient, and reliable. It provides a user-friendly interface for calculating and making decisions, and it also provides a dashboard for monitoring the system's performance.

## Project Overview
Decimatex is a Decision Support System (DSS) platform designed to automate decision analysis using MCDM (Multi-Criteria Decision Making) methods. The motivation for creating this platform came from my experience during the fourth semester, when I encountered a Decision Support System course. I needed a fast and reliable way to calculate and make decisions, but there was no available platform to perform these calculations. While AI tools sometimes failed to provide accurate answers, I decided to develop my own DSS platform to assist students, lecturers, and others in making effective decisions.

### Key Features
- User-friendly interface for calculating and making decisions
- Dashboard for monitoring the system's performance
- Integration with various databases and APIs
- Customizable and extensible
